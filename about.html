Meet Outdoorsie

Developers: Cris and Astrid


Unsure about going outside? Want to know before you go? Outdoorsie has got you covered.

Outdoorsie allows users to input their location and provides them with the current Air Quality, UV Index, and some helpful tips and precautions users can take.

Being aware of how good air quality is and how strong UV rays can get is an essential part of taking care of your health.

So take charge of your health and try Outdoorsie today! Let Outdoorsie help you enjoy your time outdoors safely.
